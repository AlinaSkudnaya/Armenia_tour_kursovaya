What awaits you?

Cliff caves

From Yerevan, the way lies to the ancient city of Khndzoresk, located on a mountain slope. You will explore the caves, literally hanging on the rocks and resembling an ancient multi-storey building, and I will tell you about the history of Khndzoresk. Here you will also take a dizzying walk along the suspension bridge over the gorge. And then in nature we will arrange a lunch with barbecue, dolma, wine and other homemade treats and enjoy the beauty and silence of these picturesque places.

Flight on "Wings of Tatev"

The longest cable car in the world is waiting for you! In 10 minutes, the modern "Wings of Tatev" will take you from the village of Galidzor to the beautiful Tatev Monastery. The road is accompanied by magnificent and breathtaking views of the Vorotan River gorge, and the highest elevation point of the cable car is 320 meters.

Tatev Monastery

The Tatev Monastery, founded in the 9th century, is considered a place of power: here human creations are harmoniously combined with the amazing beauty of nature. Examining the monastery, you will discover the history of the 14-16 centuries, when Tatev was the most important center of science and education in Armenia. Hear about the monastery's philosophers, musicians, painters, and scribes. And you will learn about the global restoration of this unique place.
