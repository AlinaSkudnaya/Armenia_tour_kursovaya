What awaits you?

Azure Lake

Sevan is a treasure of Armenia, which locals affectionately call "our sea". Picturesque mountain landscapes will amaze you with their beauty, and the fate of the reservoir will surprise you with interesting details. You will hear the legend about the formation of the lake and the history of catching the famous trout. You will go to the Sevanavank monastery on the shore of the lake, from where a magnificent view opens up.

Haghartsin Monastery

This is how guidebooks write about the monastery near the city of Dilijan. And do not cheat! Here, in the wooded valley of the reserve, you will admire the harmony of the surrounding mountains and the work of ancient architects. You will travel back to the time of the construction of Haghartsin in the 10th - 12th centuries, learn about the princely dynasty of Bagratuni and the secrets of these places. And also get acquainted with the architectural details: in this sense, the refectory is especially interesting.

Pagan Garni

Similar to a temple in Athens, like a corner of Greece in the Armenian vastness, Garni is the only pagan temple in Armenia that has survived to this day. Having been here, you will travel to pre-Christian times, to the 1st century AD, when people still prayed to the sun god on the banks of the Azat River. Next to the temple you will see the ruins of ancient baths and fortress walls, and then we will go to Geghard.
