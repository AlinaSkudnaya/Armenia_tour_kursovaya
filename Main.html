All our specialists have first-hand knowledge of the tour. We will answer all your questions about travel arrangements. We will take into account all preferences and budget to build the best route for your trip.

Confirmation of booking

After agreeing on the program, the specialist leading your request checks the availability of all places in the hotels, invites you to the office or sends a contract by mail and discusses prepayment issues. You will find out the full confirmation of the booked services within 2-3 days.
